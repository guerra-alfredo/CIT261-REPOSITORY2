TOPIC 8: Creating CSS3 Transitions and Animations in CSS and triggering them with JavaScript

1.- solar system, with two planets.

I used animation to move two planets around the sun, the animation lasts 40 seconds and is executed three times

I used different ranges of movements through @keyframes to move the planets around the sun

I used an event "onmousemove" to call and trigger some transitions through JS, this makes the planets and the sun rotate while those shape moves around the sun
